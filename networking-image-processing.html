Unleash the Power of Computer Vision Solutions

If AI enables computers to think, computer vision enables them to see, observe and understand. Computer Vision (CV) is the field of computer science that focuses on creating digital systems that can process, analyze, and make sense of visual data (images or videos) in the same way that humans do.

At WorldNetLabs INC, we take pride in designing superior CV algorithms for achieving maximum achievable accuracy and run-time performance. Our CV expertise cover the span of Image Processing, Object Detection, Advanced Encoding, Super Resolution, Content Analytics & Repurposing, AI based Media Coding and other advanced software techniques.

Our CV solutions help in content creation, analysis, distribution, consumption and user analysis. Real-time object detection and classification can form the backbone of contextual advertising. Using our facial recognition technologies, we can help you build new user experiences, personalized security solutions, personalized advertising, and others . CV techniques can enhance the AR/VR applications to sense physical objects (both surfaces and individual objects within a given physical space) in real-time and use this information to place virtual objects within the physical environment. Autonomous vehicles, and driver fatigue and driver assist system solutions use CV to make sense of surroundings, driver attention and other elements.

Our R&D engineers at World Net Labs are continuously working on the latest technologies in CV and helping our customers build unique solutions for many industries like Healthcare, Fitness and Sports, E-commerce, Retail, Marketing and Logistics.
